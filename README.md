# 💰 BACHAT - Group Expense Tracker

BACHAT is a web-based platform that simplifies group expense management. 
Built for friends, flatmates, or anyone dealing with shared finances — 
it replaces messy informal calculations with a clean, centralized system.

## 🎯 The Problem
Managing money in groups is chaotic. People forget who paid what, 
manual splits are error-prone, and asking for money back is awkward. 
BACHAT fixes all of this.

## 👥 Who Is It For
- Friend groups on trips or outings
- Flatmates splitting bills
- Anyone tired of WhatsApp money calculations

## ✨ Features
- **Group Management** – Create or join groups, log expenses with amount, category, payer, and members involved
- **Auto Split** – Automatically splits costs and updates balances in real-time
- **Bachat Tip** – Handles small extras like tips and service fees
- **Analytics** – Breaks down spending by category (food, travel, etc.)
- **Bachat Score** – Tracks your financial discipline based on logging habits and settlement consistency
- **Reminders** – Automated nudges for unsettled balances

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
- **HTML** – Structure
- **CSS** – Styling  
- **JavaScript** – Logic and real-time balance calculations

## 🚀 Getting Started
1. Clone the repo
2. Open `index.html` in your browser
3. No installations needed
